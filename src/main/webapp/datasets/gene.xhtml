Gene access



Get the number of Genes available:

    http://#{dataSets.server}/jbiowh-webservices/webservices/geneinfo/count



Get one Gene by Id (this Id is the internal JBioWH WID that change in all DB):

    http://#{dataSets.server}/jbiowh-webservices/webservices/geneinfo/#{dataSets.geneInfoWID}



Get from/to Genes by range:

    http://#{dataSets.server}/jbiowh-webservices/webservices/geneinfo/1/3



Get Gene by Gene Id:

    http://#{dataSets.server}/jbiowh-webservices/webservices/geneinfo/geneid/2947819
